My Movie Ranking By Iyanuoluwa Pelumi

My Top Three Movies in 2024

Seven Doors

This is a movie produced and directed by a Nigerian Actor by name Femi Adebayo. This beautiful masterpiece was released on Netflix. The movie centres around a king who has to save his kingdom and must go throught different rites and one of the major rites is him marrying seven wives. It became tasking for him and his family because it came at a huge cost. Couple with that, he had to pay for the misdeeds of his great grandfather who was once a king in that kingdom. This movies became my number one favorite because it of it enlightened me about what kings faced in saving their kingdom and how some can be desperate to be made king

Alagbede

This movie was also produced and directed by Femi Adebayo. The movie centers around love, trust, contentment and betrayals. A man who is ready to marry from a weathly family must honed his game to meet up with the financial demands from the lady's family. This movie display the what contentment can achieve in true love and how betrayals can set in when their wealth.

Ori (fate)

This movie was produced and directed by Muyiwa Ademola dated 2003. The movie focuses on activities and events that must come to play in our fate to come to pass. The movie is a masterpiece that should be reproduced.
